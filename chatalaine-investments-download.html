Our Foundation

A Legacy Built on Conviction and Discretion

Founded in Geneva in 1994, Chatalaine Investments has grown from a boutique family office advisory into one of the most respected private investment institutions in the world. Our philosophy is rooted in long-term thinking, rigorous research, and the unwavering belief that protecting and growing capital requires both science and wisdom.

We serve a select group of ultra-high-net-worth families, endowments, and institutional clients who demand more than performance — they demand partnership.

Absolute Confidentiality
Every client engagement is conducted with the highest standards of privacy, governed by strict fiduciary principles and a culture of discretion that has defined us for three decades.
Independent Counsel
We operate free from institutional conflicts of interest. Our advice is governed solely by your objectives, not by product mandates, commissions, or proprietary pressures.
Multigenerational Vision
True wealth management extends beyond portfolios — we advise on governance structures, succession planning, and philanthropic strategies that serve your family for generations.

I

Time is the Rarest Asset

We build portfolios designed to compound over decades, not quarters. Short-termism erodes value. Our investment horizon is defined by your legacy, not the next earnings cycle. We resist the noise of markets and focus on fundamental, enduring drivers of value creation.

II

Risk Is What You Don't See

Volatility is often mistaken for risk. True risk is permanent capital impairment. Our rigorous due diligence framework, built over 30 years, is designed to identify and stress-test the second and third-order vulnerabilities that conventional analysis misses entirely.

III

Concentration, with Conviction

Diversification without conviction is merely mediocrity distributed. We hold concentrated positions when research supports it, managed within disciplined risk parameters — because great outcomes require genuine confidence in the underlying thesis.

IV

Partnership, Not Product

We succeed only when you succeed. Our fee structures, our independence, and our culture all align us squarely with your long-term interests. Every recommendation we make, we would make for our own families.
